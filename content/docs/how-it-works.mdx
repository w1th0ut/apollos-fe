# How Apollos Works

Apollos focuses on turning volatility into productive yield with a structured vault system.

## At A Glance

1. User enters via dashboard.
2. Capital is routed into selected strategy vaults.
3. Position is monitored through protocol health and liquidity metrics.
4. User can rebalance, bridge, or withdraw from the same interface.

## Components

### Earn

The Earn section is the primary entry for strategy allocation.

### DEX Pools

DEX Pools provide visibility on pool composition, APR, volume, and fee signals.

### My Balances

My Balances tracks active positions and recent activity history in one place.

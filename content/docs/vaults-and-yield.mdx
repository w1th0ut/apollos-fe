# Vaults and Yield

Vaults are strategy containers designed for specific market conditions.

## Strategy Inputs

- TVL and pool depth
- Volume-to-liquidity efficiency
- Fee generation trend
- Position health metrics

## What Affects Yield

### Market Volatility

Higher volatility can increase fee opportunities, but risk management remains critical.

### Liquidity Balance

Imbalanced liquidity can affect execution efficiency and realized returns.

### User Behavior

Frequent switching between vaults can reduce strategy compounding continuity.

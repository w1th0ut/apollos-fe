# Bridge and Zap

Bridge is designed to simplify moving assets into strategy vaults without manual multi-step routing.

## Standard Flow

1. Choose source and destination network route.
2. Enter deposit amount.
3. Select destination vault.
4. Confirm route preview and fees.
5. Execute bridge and zap transaction.

## Route Preview

Before submitting, users can review:

- estimated bridge fee
- expected destination amount
- estimated minted vault position

## Operational Notes

Use this flow when your funds are not already on the target network.
